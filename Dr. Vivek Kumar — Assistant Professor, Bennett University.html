HPCNLP Lab
High Performance Computing for Big Data and NLP Research Lab

The exponential growth of data has stimulated a new specialisation in software: data-intensive systems. Our mission is to design, implement, and evaluate algorithms and techniques for applications that are scalable, reliable, and community-serving. We collaborate with labs across institutions to advance the state of the art in HPC, NLP, and AI.

  • BE/BS/B.Tech./M.Tech./MS in Computer Science or related fields
  • Background in Artificial Intelligence or Big Data
  • Programming skills in C / Python / Java / CUDA-C / CUDA-Python
